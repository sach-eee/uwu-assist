We are on a mission to help

The main purpose of this web page is to find out the basic needs of the students of Uva Wellassa university of Sri Lanka. Our aim is to find solutions to the problems according to medical,Transport,cafeteria and Hostel. Using UWU Assists we can check and order the food menu available in the canteen through the cafeteria service. Also, you can find out the times of university transport as well as the public transport, contact a private wheel only if necessary in case of emergency, you can do it easily through our transport service.
