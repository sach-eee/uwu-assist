First aid Information to help you during a medical emergency.


FOR FAINTING

FOR FAINTING


If you feel faint,


Lie down or sit down. Place your head betwee your knees.

If someone else faints,


Position the person on his or her back. Check for the breathing.




FOR INJURIES

FOR INJURIES


Wounds first Aid,



 Stop the bleeding.
 Rinse the wound.
 Apply an antibiotic.
 Cover with a bandage.
 Change it every day.





FOR ANKLE INJURY

FOR ANKLE INJURY


Self Care,


Rest. Avoid activities that cause pain, swelling or discomfort.
Ice. Use an ice pack or ice slush bath immediately for 15 to 20 minutes and repeat every two to three hours while you're awake.





FOR SOFT TISSUE INJURIES

FOR SOFT TISSUE INJURIES


PRICE - Treatment for soft tissue injuries and closed fractures,


P - Protect the injury (stop using the injured limb, pad to protect.
R - Rest the injury
I - Ice- apply a wrapped ice pack
C - Comfortable support(Apply a supportive bandage)
E - Elevate to reduce swelling


FOR STOMACHACHES

FOR STOMACHACHES


Self care


Sit or lie down in a comfortable position. Try to get an idea on probable cause. Drink boiled and cooled water. Place hot water bottle/heating pads. Take antacids for burning sensation. In case of vomiting, sip ORS. Take mild laxative if constipation persists. Avoid aspirin and ibuprofen.
